The Problem

Every 10 seconds, a child dies from hunger.

Hunger kills more people each year than AIDS, malaria and terrorism combined.

One in every eight people sleeps hungry each night.

About 21 million tonnes of wheat are wasted in India and 50% of all food across the world meets the same fate and never reaches the needy.

According to the World Resources Institute, 24% of all the water used for agriculture is lost through food waste every year.

India wastes Rs 244 crore worth of food a day.
